Setúbal & Azeitão Wine Tour

Discover Lisbon’s iconic landmarks, historic neighborhoods, and breathtaking viewpoints in a compact journey through the city’s timeless charm.

DurationAprox. 8 hours
Group Size1 to 7 passengers
Starts FromYour hotel in Lisbon/Sintra/Cascais
PriceFrom 525€ (includes Winery Tour and Wine Tasting)

Suggested Itinerary

Pick up at requested hotel/apartment/cruise at 09:00 or another time suggested by you

Drive to Setúbal

Visit the Livramento Market, one of the best farmers markets in the world

Wander with your Guide around the city center of Setúbal where you can try some of the most popular local foods (Bifana and Fried Cuttlefish)

Drive to some of the best viewpoints of the Arrábida Hill

Lunch - We will recommend the best options according to your needs (not included)

Tour and Wine Tasting at José Maria da Fonseca (included)

Drop off at the starting point or another place that is convenient for you

Good to Know
