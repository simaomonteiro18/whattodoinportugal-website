Algarve Full Day Private Tour

Experience the Algarve’s golden cliffs, stunning beaches, and charming seaside villages along Portugal’s spectacular southern coast.

DurationApprox. 12 hours
Group Size1 to 7 passengers
Starts FromYour hotel in Lisbon/Sintra/Cascais
PriceFrom 790€ for 2 pax

Suggested Itinerary

Pick up at requested hotel/apartment/cruise at the requested time

Drive to Algar Seco rock formations where you will see the Boneca Cave

Optional : Drive to Portimão for a boat ride to the beaches and caves of Carvoeiro and Benagil (2 hours - paid separately | only operates with good sea conditions)

Lunch

Explore the Ponta da Piedade cliffs and take amazing pictures

Walk the boardwalks of Lagos and see the Camilo beach

After this you can either spend some time relaxing at a beach or go back to one of the places you saw from the boat to see them from another perspective

Drive back to the starting point of to any other place close-by of your choice

If you have a specific place you would like to add on your tour we are open to that
