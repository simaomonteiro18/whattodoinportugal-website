Lisbon Full Day Private Tour

Dive deep into Lisbon’s rich history, vibrant culture, and stunning viewpoints across the city of seven hills.

DurationAprox. 8 hours
Group Size1 to 7 passengers
Starts FromYour hotel in Lisbon/Sintra/Cascais
PriceFrom 400€

Suggested Itinerary

Pick up at requested hotel/apartment/cruise at 09:00 (recommended) or another time convenient for you

Drive around the central part of the city (Rossio) and go up the main avenue (Avenida da Liberdade) to reach the top of Park Edward VII, where you can admire a beautiful view over the central part of Lisbon

Stop at Sé (Lisbon Cathedral), the oldest church in Lisbon (Free admission)

Drive to one of the highest viewpoints of the city (Senhora do Monte) where you will have almost a 360º view over the city, the bridge and the river

Drive down the hills and see some incredible monuments like the S. Vicente de Fora Church and the National Pantheon.

Free time in Alfama, for a small walk around the oldest neighbourhood in the city

Stop at S. Pedro de Alcântara viewpoint and the S. Roque Church, both in Bairro Alto

Free time for lunch (we can recommend a local restaurant - no tourist traps!)

Drive to Belém, taking the road along the river

Visit to the Jerónimos Monastery

Taste the famous custard tarts from Lisbon (Pastéis de Belém) at the original factory

Stop at the Tower of Belém (optional visit / under renovations until late 2026)

Stop at the Discoveries Monument (Padrão dos Descobrimentos)

Head on to the new part of the city where the EXPO 98 took place and see the Oceanarium and the longest suspension bridge in Europe (Vasco da Gama Bridge). You might even have some time to go shopping in the Vasco da Gama Shopping Center or ride the cable car along the river

Drop off at the starting point or another place that is convenient for you
